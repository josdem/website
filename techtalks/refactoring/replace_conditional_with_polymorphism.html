Replace conditional with polymorphism

The essence of polymorphism is that it allows you to avoid writting an explicit conditional when you have objects whose behavior varies depending on their types.
Polymorphism gives you many advantages. The biggest gain occurs when this same set of conditions appears in many places in the program. if you want to add a new type, you have to find and update all the conditionals, but with sublcasses you just create a new subclass and provide the appropiate methods.
Before you can begin with Replace Conditional With Polymorphism you need to have the necessary inheritance structure.
